Capadoccia

Cappadocia, a mesmerizing region in central Turkey, captivates with its otherworldly landscapes and fairy-tale-like rock formations. Carved by nature and human history, Cappadocia's unique charm unfolds in its ancient cave dwellings, surreal chimneys, and vibrant hot air balloon-filled skies, creating an enchanting destination that seamlessly blends history, culture, and natural wonders.

Where to stay?

Museum Hotel

A luxurious cave hotel offering unique rooms with stunning views. Features a world-class restaurant and a beautiful outdoor pool. Located in Uchisar, near famous rock formations.

Activities in Cappadocia

Hot Air Balloon Ride

Cappadocia is famous for its stunning landscapes, and a hot air balloon ride is one of the best ways to experience it. You can enjoy breathtaking views of fairy chimneys, rock formations, and valleys during sunrise.

Goreme Open-Air Museum

This UNESCO World Heritage site features rock-cut churches and monasteries with well-preserved frescoes. It provides a glimpse into the region's rich religious history.

Horseback Riding

Riding a horse through the valleys and fairy chimneys is a popular and enjoyable activity in Cappadocia.


Important things to know

Unique Landscape:
Cappadocia is renowned for its surreal and unique landscape, characterized by fairy-tale-like chimney rock formations and cave dwellings.


Hot Air Balloon Rides:
Experience the breathtaking beauty of Cappadocia from above with enchanting hot air balloon rides, offering panoramic views of the otherworldly terrain.


Cave Churches and Frescoes:
Explore ancient cave churches adorned with intricate frescoes, providing a glimpse into the rich history of the region.


Underground Cities:
Delve into the mysterious underground cities of Cappadocia, such as Derinkuyu and Kaymaklı, showcasing impressive subterranean structures used for shelter and storage.


Local Handicrafts:
Immerse yourself in the vibrant local culture by exploring Cappadocia's markets, where you can find unique handicrafts, carpets, and traditional pottery.


Goreme Open-Air Museum:
Visit the Goreme Open-Air Museum, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, housing rock-cut churches and monasteries with well-preserved frescoes.


Cappadocian Cuisine:
Indulge in the delicious Cappadocian cuisine, known for specialties like testi kebab, manti (Turkish dumplings), and unique grape-based dishes.


Hiking Trails:
Discover the region's natural beauty by hiking through scenic trails, including the Love Valley and Rose Valley, offering stunning vistas and rock formations.


Fairy Chimneys:
Marvel at the iconic fairy chimneys, towering rock formations shaped by centuries of natural erosion, creating a surreal and picturesque landscape.


Hospitality of the Locals:
Experience warm hospitality from the friendly locals, known for their welcoming nature and willingness to share the beauty and stories of their region.


Remember to check travel advisories and stay informed about local regulations and customs during your visit to Cappadocia.
